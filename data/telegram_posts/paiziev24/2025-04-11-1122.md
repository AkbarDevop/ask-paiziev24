Just wrapped up an insightful few days at TIA 2025 in San Antonio, TX — great energy, great people, and valuable conversations shaping the future of logistics.

Here are a few key takeaways:

🔹 AI is entering the broker side of logistics faster than ever — with tools being built to streamline operations and reduce manual tasks.

🔹 There’s noticeable uncertainty in the industry, largely driven by changing tariff policies and economic pressure.

🔹 As margins tighten, the industry is doubling down on efficiency and automation — everyone’s asking: *how can we do more with less?*

🔹 One of the most talked-about issues: fraud. It's growing, it's real, and it’s forcing many companies to rethink how they validate partners and secure transactions.
