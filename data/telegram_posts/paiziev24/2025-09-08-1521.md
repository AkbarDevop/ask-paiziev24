Yo folks! We’re expanding 🙂. We’re looking for AI Product Engineer to join our growing team.

Responsibilities:
- Build and integrate LLM-powered workflows into production systems.
- Develop and deploy AI-driven features (Voice AI, agents, RAG pipelines, knowledge graphs).
- Work with vector databases & graph databases to enable memory, reasoning, and retrieval.
- Design backend services and APIs (with occasional frontend work in React/JS to ship features).
- Actively communicate progress, blockers, and new ideas with the team.

Requirements:
- 3+ years in software engineering
- Strong knowledge of Python and JavaScript
- Hands-on experience building and integrating LLM-powered workflows
- Knowledge of ML concepts and practical experience with:
- Voice AI frameworks/services (e.g. LiveKit, Pipecat, Daily.co (http://Daily.co/), Agora, Janus, or audio/voice APIs like ElevenLabs, Rime, Deepgram, Speechmatics, Whisper (STT), Coqui TTS)

- Vector databases (e.g. Pinecone, Weaviate, Qdrant, Milvus, Vespa, or FAISS)
- Graph databases (e.g. Neo4j, TigerGraph, ArangoDB, or Dgraph for knowledge graph integration)
- Ability to quickly learn/adapt to new tech (we move fast!)
- Bonus: experience with cloud platforms (we use GCP for compute & storage, Azure for inference)
- Proactive, full ownership mindset, and strong communication skills
